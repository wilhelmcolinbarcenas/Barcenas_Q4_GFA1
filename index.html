Philippine Red Cross

Why support them?

There are numerous reasons as to why I support this charity organization. They assist in disaster relief projects, as well as health emergencies. Since it has many organizations all over the world, including in the Philippines, it is able to reach even vulnerable people who can't be supported by their own governments. Lastly, Red Cross is neutral, and their only missions is to protect lives, meaning they don't discriminate for any reason.
